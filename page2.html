Noah Lyles

This guy, Noah Lyles, is the fastest 200m sprinter of our generation. We the prs(personal record) of 9.79 in the 100 and 19.31 in the 200m. He was undeafeated for a stretch of races and even claimed the American Record in the 200 and holds the record for the most sub 20 200m races of all time. He is even a 4x world champion in the 200 while also winning the 100m gold medal at the biggest stage of sports entertainment, The Olympics.
